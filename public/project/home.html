Welcome to Life1st! Life1st was created in order to help people reduce their impact on the planet by limiting their carbon-dioxide emissions and helping them to live more sustainably with our planet and human, animal and all other life on first place.

Once you create an account, you will receive a short list of suggestions for small changes in your life that could have big impact on the planet.

You will be able to choose what you want to commit to. You will furthermore become part of a network of new friends to share your commitments with.

We promise to be by your side all along the way by creating new suggestions, helping you find more people to realize your commitments with and supporting you every day on the path to a cleaner and healthier home not only for humans but for all creatures!

Let's do this together!
